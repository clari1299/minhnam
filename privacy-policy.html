Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MINH NAM GROUP's Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and games (collectively, the "Apps").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

By downloading, registering with, or using our Apps, you consent to the collection, use, disclosure and transfer of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our Apps.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our Apps, including:

2.1 Personal Data

Personal Data refers to information that can be used to identify you specifically. We may collect the following Personal Data:

  • Contact Information: Such as your email address, name, and phone number when you register for an account or contact our support team.
  • Profile Information: Such as username, password, profile picture, and preferences.
  • Transaction Information: Such as purchase records, billing address, and payment information when you make in-app purchases.
  • Authentication Information: Such as login credentials and account verification details.

2.2 Usage Data

Usage Data refers to information that is automatically collected when you use our Apps. This may include:

  • Device Information: Such as your mobile device's unique device identifier (UDID), device type, operating system, and mobile network information.
  • Log Information: Such as your app activity, app crashes, system activity, hardware settings, browser type, browser language, date and time of your request and referral URL.
  • Game Progress: Such as your game scores, achievements, and progress in our games.
  • Interaction Information: Such as information about your interactions with our Apps, including features you use and time spent on the Apps.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect and process information about your precise or approximate location.

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Apps and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

These technologies are used for the following purposes:

  • To enable certain functions of the Apps
  • To provide analytics
  • To store your preferences
  • To personalize your experience
  • To assist in our marketing efforts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Apps
  • To process your transactions and manage your account
  • To personalize your experience with our Apps
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To inform you about changes to our Apps, products, services, and offers
  • To prevent fraud, enforce our terms and conditions, and protect our rights and the rights of other users
  • To measure performance and ensure technical functionality of our Apps
  • To analyze your usage of our Apps to improve our products and services
  • To display advertisements tailored to your interests

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, payment processing, customer service, email delivery, and auditing.
  • Business Partners: Third parties with whom we may partner to offer you certain products, services, or promotions.
  • Advertisers and Advertising Networks: Third-party advertising companies that may use tracking technologies to collect information about your activities on our Apps to provide advertisements tailored to your interests.
  • Analytics Providers: Third parties that collect and analyze information about your use of our Apps to help us improve our products and services.
  • Corporate Affiliates: Our parent company, subsidiaries, joint ventures, or other companies under common control with us.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our files as soon as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@minhnamgroup.com.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction of processing of your personal information
  • Data portability (receiving your personal information in a structured, commonly used format)
  • Objection to processing of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@minhnamgroup.com.

7.1 Account Information

You can review and change your personal information by logging into your account settings. You may also send us an email at privacy@minhnamgroup.com to request access to, correct, or delete any personal information that you have provided to us.

7.2 Push Notifications

You can opt out of receiving push notifications through your device settings. Please note that opting out of receiving push notifications may impact your experience with our Apps.

7.3 Location Information

You can choose whether or not to allow our Apps to collect and use real-time information about your device's location through your device settings. If you block the use of location information, some parts of our Apps may become inaccessible or not function properly.

7.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of our Apps may become inaccessible or not function properly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside Vietnam and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the information, including personal information, to Vietnam and process it there. Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our Apps may contain links to third-party websites and services. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site or service. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit and every service you use.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

10. Advertising and Analytics

10.1 Advertising

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you use our Apps. These companies may use information about your visits to our and other websites and apps, in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

10.2 Analytics

We use third-party analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to help us measure traffic and usage trends for our Apps. These tools collect information sent by your device or our Apps, including the web pages you visit, add-ons, and other information that assists us in improving our Apps.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Your continued use of our Apps after we post any modifications to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.
